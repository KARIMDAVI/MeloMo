## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette
- **Primary Warm Accents:** Gold (#DAA520), Copper (#B87333), Burnt Orange (#CC5500)
- **Backgrounds:** Warm Black (#1A1410), Vinyl Black (#2D2D2D), Deep Navy (#1F2937)
- **Text/Neutrals:** Cream (#F5F1E8), Beige (#E8DCC8), Light Gray (#D4D0C5)
- **Mood-Reactive:** 8 mood colors (Energetic, Chill, Focused, Emotional, Romantic, Social, Magical, Melancholy)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** All-caps, bold, 40pt down to 20pt, +1% letter spacing
- **Body:** Regular, 16-14pt, +0.15% letter spacing
- **9 total type scales** optimized for all-caps display

### Components
1. **WarmMoodCard** — Sound wave visualization, silhouette effect, rounded
2. **VinylRecordCard** — Spinning vinyl with grooves, golden center label
3. **WarmNowPlayingCard** — Large vinyl showcase with golden controls
4. **WarmPrimaryButton** — Copper/gold gradient, all-caps, glowing shadow
5. **WarmCategoryChip** — Warm-themed filter pills

### Animations
- **Vinyl Spin:** 4 seconds linear, infinite loop
- **Card Spring:** 0.35s response, 0.7 damping
- **Button Press:** 0.15s scale + opacity
- **Golden Glow Pulse:** 1.5s infinite opacity pulse
